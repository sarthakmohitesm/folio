Avantika Kansal

Avantika Kansal is a Journalist, Political Commentator, and Content Creator working at the intersection of national politics, policy, and geopolitics. With a sharp analytical approach and a passion for making complex issues accessible, she brings critical insights to public discourse.

Avvanteeka – A platform dedicated to politics, public policy, and international affairs, where Avantika breaks down contemporary developments and offers nuanced analysis for her audience.

Review with Avantika Kansal – A conversational space where she explores books, ideas, and stories with thought leaders and changemakers, providing viewers with deep insights and engaging discussions.

Avantika also hosts The Global Lens with Avantika, a show that explores diverse perspectives on international relations and policy shifts, and Chapters and Curtains with Avantika Kansal, a platform which brings forward inspiring personal journeys, literature, and creative voices.

YouTube Channels

In addition, she regularly hosts seminars at universities and colleges and actively participates in ground events wherever her expertise is required. Avantika has studied Public Policy at IIM Calcutta and is a Geopolitics Fellow with IMPRI (Impact and Policy Research Institute).

In the past, she has worked with leading Indian think tanks and continues to remain actively engaged with them, contributing to research, policy dialogues, and strategic discussions on national and global issues. Through her journalism, content creation, and policy engagements, Avantika Kansal bridges the gap between governance, geopolitics, and citizen understanding, creating narratives that inform, inspire, and provoke thought.
